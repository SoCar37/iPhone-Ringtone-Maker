Installing on iPhone
1. AirDrop the .wav to your iPhone
2. Open GarageBand and import the audio
3. Tap My Songs → long-press the project → Share → Ringtone
4. Name it and tap Export — it goes straight to Settings
5. Delete the .wav from your Files app — it's no longer needed
Runs entirely in your browser — no data is uploaded or shared.
