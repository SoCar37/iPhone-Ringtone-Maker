Installing on iPhone
GarageBand (easiest): AirDrop the .m4r to your iPhone, open GarageBand, import the audio, then export as a ringtone directly to Settings.

iTunes / Finder: Drag the .m4r into iTunes, sync your iPhone, then go to Settings → Sounds → Ringtone.
